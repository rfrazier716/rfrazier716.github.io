About Ryan Frazier

Welcome to my site! I'm an Optical Engineer located in Boston, designing ultra-low-power environmental sensors. Additionally, I write and maintain a large set of software tools used to model, capture, and analyze sensor data.

Outside of work you will typically find me running or tinkering with my current side project. Most of my projects focus on computer graphics and various ways of using Python to simplify my life.
